Welcome to Kru Bird Muay Thai

My life passion is to pass on Muay Thai and Muay Boran knowledge to anyone who wants to learn. I want to pass on Muay Thai in its purest form to as many people as possible to ensure the art is never lost.

Muay Thai is Life

I had my first Muay Thai fight at just 8 years old, growing up in a small village in the Chumphon province I had a thirst for fighting that I couldn’t explain… cut forward 20 years and 100 fights later I discovered that my father too was a Muay Thai enthusiast; It was then that I realised that Muay Thai is in my blood.

Government Accredited

I am a government accredited Muay Thai Trainer with both C and B-Licence certification. This rare opportunity to study under some of Thailand’s greatest Muay Thai professionals has awarded me the opportunity to pass on valuable knowledge to my students. A trainer to the stars as well, I have trained sporting champions, actors, and even eccentric influencers.
